ABOUT US

ADDING THE EXTRAORDINARY
TO EVERYDAY LIFE

At Green Interiors we bring together the finest materials, traditional artisanal craftsmanship, and an approach thats wholly bespoke to your ideas to create the best in British luxury interiors.

OUR BELIEFS

We believe in helping you define your dreams, before realising that inspiration with care and imagination. Its about fusing your desires, tastes and needs with modern design innovation and traditional craftsmans passion for perfection. By collaborating creatively, we will make the setting for your everyday truly extraordinary.

OUR CLIENTS

Our clients are as diverse as the dreams they share with us. For us, its about saying yes to possibility and thinking far beyond the confines of convention. We embrace individuality, eclecticism and whimsy, adding creativity and the very best of British craftsmanship to create truly special places to live.

From the contemporary to the traditional, the unparalleled design of our collections will bring opulence and creativity to your home. Whether its a kitchen, bedroom, dressing room or an office, well help you to create the ultimate space to entertain, relax and elevate the everyday.

BRITISH CRAFTSMANSHIP

Our workshop in the north of England is a UK Centre of Excellence. There, generations of artisan craftsmen and women combine precision technology with 300 year-old cabinetry and finishing techniques. Its home to some of the finest skilled artisans currently working today. This is where we continue to create exquisite furniture that will bring warmth and life to your living space for generations to come.

We take great pride in choosing - and using - only the finest materials. Our team are tasked with continually discovering and responsibly sourcing ever-more unique materials from around the world, ready to perfect every creation. Our veneers are equally at home in a Bentley or Rolls Royce, as they are gracing our cabinetry. Furthermore, where possible, we support small UK manufacturers who - like us - have a no compromise policy when it comes to quality.

WHERE CRAFT MEETS ART

Every piece of cabinetry, panelling and furniture is a meeting of precision technology with centuries old artisan techniques such as marquetry and burr veneering - true artistry synonymous with great British craftsmanship.

As a UK Centre of Excellence, we centre on wood craft from managed resources. Furthermore, we maintain a biomass converter to utilise all offcuts to provide heat for the workshop and underline our environmentally friendly ethos.
