ABOUT THE CHIEF DESIGNER

Gary Logan, a seasoned brand designer hailing from the creative hub of Melbourne, made a pivotal decision to expand his horizons globally.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling through design, John has crafted identities for renowned brands across various industries. His journey began in the vibrant streets of Sydney, where he honed his skills and developed a unique aesthetic that seamlessly blends innovation with tradition.

After establishing himself as a sought-after talent in the Australian design scene, John set his sights on the United States, eager to explore new opportunities and collaborate with diverse talents. His approach to branding is characterized by a deep understanding of market trends, a commitment to excellence, and a relentless drive to push boundaries.

Having worked with multinational corporations, startups, and everything in between, John's portfolio speaks volumes about his ability to translate ideas into visually compelling narratives that resonate with audiences worldwide. Whether it's revamping a legacy brand or launching a disruptive startup, John's expertise shines through in every project he undertakes.

In his free time, you can find John exploring art galleries for inspiration, experimenting with new design techniques, or simply enjoying a flat white at his favorite local cafe. With a reputation for delivering exceptional results and a genuine passion for his craft, Gary Logan continues to make waves in the competitive world of brand design, leaving an indelible mark wherever his creativity takes him.
