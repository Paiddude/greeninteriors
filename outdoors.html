OUTDOORS

INTRODUCING THE GARDEN KITCHEN BY Green Interiors, THE ULTIMATE STATEMENT OUTDOOR KITCHEN.

Utilising the brand’s signature materiality, craftsmanship and unparalleled design prowess, the Garden Kitchen, is an ode to the prestige of living life well and a reflection of the true artistry involved in luxury furniture design.

“Inspired by the glamour of classic yachts, our outdoor cabinetry has been constructed using the processes, materials and techniques employed in creating some of the greatest and most recognisable yachts in the world.

Whilst learning from the technology and design seen within the marine environment, we have ensured that our Garden Kitchen has remained true to our core essence - opulence through exceptional design.”

- Oliver Deadman, Head of Design

BRINGING EXCEPTIONALLY INSPIRED OPULENCE, OUTDOORS.

  • Elevating your outdoor dining space for the epitome of alfresco dining, the Garden Kitchen is a distinguished collection, boasting refined features, oozing debonair scale and grandeur.

  • The cabinetry reflects one of the most identifiable elements in many yachts - the pinstriped deck. Reimagined through inlaying ebonised Iroko into sections of the drawer fronts, this detail showcases our expertise in marquetry, a skill synonymous with Green Interiors.

  • In a unique manner for Green Interiors, our Garden Kitchen cabinetry has been designed to sit off the ground. This allows access for cleaning and removing surface water, and elevates the cabinetry from the dampness of the ground below.

“It is long established that a Green Interiors designs are timeless, and it was imperative that this core value was at the heart of our first Garden Kitchen.”

If you have a project you would like to discuss with us, our team is at your disposal and looks forward to answering your questions.
