Our Workshop

PASSING ON TIME HONOURED TECHNIQUES

We are proud to be introducing a series of apprenticeships to maintain precious standards and impart knowledge to the next generation of skilled artisans. True British craftsmanship paired with superior materials ensures all projects are ingrained with integrity and our passion to produce the very best in luxurious living.

MATERIALS & SKILLS

We take great pride in choosing – and using – only the finest materials. Our team are tasked with continually discovering and responsibly sourcing ever-more unique materials from around the world, ready to perfect every Green Interiors creation. Our veneers are equally at home in a Bentley or Rolls Royce as they are gracing our cabinetry. Furthermore, where possible, we support small UK manufacturers who – like us – have a ‘no compromise’ policy when it comes to quality.

WH

Every piece of cabinetry, paneling and furniture is a meeting of precision with centuries old artisan techniques such as marquetry and burr veneering – true synonymous with great British craftsmanship.

RESPONSIBLE TO PEOPLE AND THE PLANET

As a UK Centre of Excellence, we centre on wood craft from managed resources. Furthermore, we maintain a biomass converter to utilise all offcuts to provide heat for the workshop and underline our environmentally friendly ethos.
